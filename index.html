Why Amcha Ghar

Every child deserves to have a childhood filled with happy memories.
Amcha Ghar is a non-profit organization that understands the
adversities faced by many young girls. We have worked together and
have devised solutions to aid them in the best possible way. Since
1996, we have been involved in their holistic development. Over
100’s of orphan girls have been provided with the much needed care,
love and support at our orphanage. We empower the orphanage
children by working closely with various institutes. We are helping
orphans by providing them with good food, quality education, psycho
-social support, monthly health check-up and treatment if any, sports
/ recreational and extra-curricular interventions for their inclusive
growth at the girls orphanage.
We have a long way to go in creating a safe haven for young
children. Helping orphans lead a merrier life is what we aim for. With
your kindness and donation, these young children will definitely find
their path to a fulfilling life. While there are many orphanages in India
aiding these beautiful kids, many still need to be cared for. We hope a
day comes where every child is looked after, because every kid
deserves to live a more dignified life filled with happy moments.
